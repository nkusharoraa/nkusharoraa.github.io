About Me

Hello! I am Ankush Arora, a Mechanical Engineer specializing in steering systems and suspension design for commercial vehicles at Bajaj Auto. With extensive expertise in Siemens NX and Teamcenter, I optimize steering and suspension hard points, develop innovative mechanisms, and perform ADAMS analysis to ensure top performance and safety.

In addition to my engineering endeavors, I have a profound interest in data-driven approaches to problem-solving, including operations research and inventory management. I am passionate about leveraging technology to drive efficiency and innovation in the automotive industry.

As a results-oriented engineer, I thrive on exploring the intersection of data analysis and machine learning, particularly through my interest in Operations Research. My proficiency in C++, data structures, and algorithms enables me to tackle problems with meticulous attention to detail and efficiency.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I find joy in creative writing, sports like tennis and cricket, and dramatics. These hobbies not only enrich my personal life but also foster a well-rounded perspective that enhances my time management and problem-solving abilities. I believe in the power of teaching to drive personal growth and actively share my knowledge and insights with others.

With a data-driven mindset and a passion for solving real-world challenges, I welcome new opportunities to expand my horizons and collaborate with like-minded individuals. I am committed to delivering projects within schedule and making a meaningful impact in the world.
