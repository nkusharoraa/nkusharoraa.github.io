About Me

Hello! I am Ankush Arora, a Mechanical Engineer specializing in steering systems and suspension design for commercial vehicles at Bajaj Auto. With expertise in Siemens NX and Teamcenter, I optimize steering and suspension hard points, develop innovative mechanisms, and perform ADAMS analysis to ensure top performance and safety.

Besides engineering, I have a keen interest in exploring data-driven approaches to problem-solving, including operations research and inventory management. I'm passionate about leveraging technology to drive efficiency and innovation in the automotive industry.
